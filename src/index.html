PropIntel

PropIntel is a smart web application that aggregates real estate listings for rentals and sales across multiple platforms. It enhances this with rich historical data, providing insights into pricing trends, land ownership, property size, and area metrics. By combining real-time listings with long-term data, PropIntel helps users make more informed decisions when buying or renting properties. It’s designed for individuals, investors, and analysts who want a clear, data-driven view of the real estate landscape.
